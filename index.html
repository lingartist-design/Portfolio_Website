About Me

AI Artist & Prompt Engineer | 2023-2025

AI Experience

As the lead AI Artist at SkyCastle, I specialized in generating high-quality character images using AI-powered tools like Stable Diffusion, Inpaint, and Photoshop. My role focused on prompt engineering, character styling, and anatomy corrections.

I consistently generated and refined over 100 characters daily for more than 10 collections, contributing to the visual identity of SkyCastle's character database across multiple fashion collections.

My work involved transforming initial AI generations into polished final images through detailed corrections, color adjustments, and anatomical refinements.

Prompting Methodology

Techniques for generating high-quality AI character images

Positive Prompt

(masterpiece:1.2, best quality), (real picture), (perfect eyes, looking at viewer), (realistic), ((stood in front of a simple_white_background))

Negative Prompt

(worst quality, low quality:1.4), (EasyNegative, ng_deepnegative_v1_75t, bad-hands-5:1.2), watermark, letterbox, text, writing, signature, (overexposure, backlight, underexposure, backlit, blurry, depth of field, out of focus, blur), (outlines, drawing, cartoon, painting, crosshatching, sketch, mutilated face, bad anatomy, open mouth, parted lips, earrings, deformed irises, deformed pupils, cross-eyed)
